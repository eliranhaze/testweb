I am a Motwani Postdoctoral Fellow in Stanford's Computer Science theory group, hosted by Omer Reingold.

My research lies within Theory of Computation, mainly computational complexity, randomness in computation and explicit constructions. More specifically, I am interesterd in pseudorandomness, derandomization, and error-correcting codes.

Before coming to Stanford, I was a postdoctoral researcher in UT Austin's Algorithms and Computational Theory Group hosted by Dana Moshkovitz and David Zuckerman. I received my PhD from Tel-Aviv University, where I was fortunate to have Amnon Ta-Shma as my advisor.
